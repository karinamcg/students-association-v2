Meet your student presidential team!

Your newly elected CitySA Presidential Team are now in office and here to represent you! Every spring is the Big Student Elections, we let you vote for the students who will make sure the student voice is heard at the highest level at City of Glasgow College.

The Student President is a full time position and the Vice Presidents are in part time. Each role is unique and they will offer support in different ways. Find out more about your representatives:
