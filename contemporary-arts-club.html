Contemporary Arts Club

Join us weekly, every Wednesday from 5-7pm at City Campus, in the Students' Association space on the second floor.

About our Club

The Contemporary Arts Club is a place where we can all be creative for a couple of hours! Whether it's drawing, painting, knitting, sculpting etc bring your craft to the club and share your art with us!

Is it free?

It is free to join, but please bring your own materials. There is nowhere to store resources, so please keep in mind that you will have to take home what you bring.
